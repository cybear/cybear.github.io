You can easily generate HTTP Archive files when visiting an Open Device Lab.

  1. Install WireShark
  2. Choose to sniff on your wireless interface.
  3. Define a filter to sniff on. "HTTP" is normally enough. Apply.
  4. Optional - connect your laptop by cable to the Internet (if you need access to the Internet).
  5. Share your connection. (On Mac: Network > Create Network.)
  6. Connect all devices to your network.
  7. Visit your site!
  8. When your session is over, choose "File > Save as", choose format "Wireshark/TCPDump libcap".
  9. Upload your file toÂ http://pcapperf.appspot.com/
  10. You can see a nice waterfall and download your HAR!
